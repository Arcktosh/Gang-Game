# Community Rules

Current as of Feature Pass 59.

These rules are the operating baseline for public beta testing.

## Rules

1. Keep gameplay fictional. Do not encourage real-world crime, harm, harassment, or unsafe behavior.
2. Do not threaten, dox, stalk, impersonate, or target other players or operators.
3. Do not exploit bugs, automate gameplay, evade rate limits, operate bots, or abuse idempotency/security boundaries.
4. Do not trade accounts, sell access, manipulate entitlements, or attempt payment fraud.
5. Do not spam messages, reports, appeals, shop listings, faction invites, or newspaper interactions.
6. Report bugs, unsafe messages, exploit paths, economy anomalies, and moderation mistakes through the available report and appeal tools.

## Operator workflow

Operators should use admin RBAC, moderation reports, appeals, enforcement records, audit logs, transparency summaries, and backup/restore runbooks instead of direct database changes whenever possible.

## Enforcement ladder

Recommended beta ladder:

1. warning or content removal;
2. temporary message/action restriction;
3. temporary account or character enforcement;
4. economy correction or rollback for exploit abuse;
5. permanent account action for severe abuse or evasion.
