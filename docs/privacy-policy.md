# Privacy Policy Draft

Current as of Feature Pass 59.

DrugDeal Game is a fictional text-based browser MMO MVP. This privacy policy draft exists so public beta testers and operators have a clear baseline before live testing begins. It should be reviewed by counsel before commercial launch.

## Data categories

The MVP may store:

- account identifiers and authentication/session state;
- character names, resources, status, progression, inventory, factions, shops, and gameplay history;
- messages, reports, moderation decisions, appeals, enforcement records, and admin audit logs;
- operational telemetry such as request IDs, route names, status codes, timing metadata, and error diagnostics;
- entitlement and cosmetic records for non-pay-to-win monetization tests.

## Purpose

Data is used to operate persistent gameplay, authenticate users, restore sessions, prevent abuse, moderate content, investigate support issues, validate releases, balance the economy, and preserve backup/restore continuity.

## Payments

Live payment processing is intentionally disabled in the MVP. Product catalog, entitlement, and cosmetic records exist for a future fair-monetization layer, but checkout remains provider-disabled until legal, refund, support, and provider adapter work is complete.

## Moderation and safety

Authorized operators may review reports, messages, gameplay records, audit logs, enforcement actions, and appeals to keep the beta safe, fair, and reviewable.

## Retention and deletion

Production retention periods are not finalized. Before public beta, define retention windows for logs, sessions, messages, reports, backups, and deleted accounts.

## Pre-launch action

Before public launch, replace this draft with jurisdiction-appropriate legal text and publish it through `/privacy`.
