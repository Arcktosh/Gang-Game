# Terms of Service Draft

Current as of Feature Pass 59.

DrugDeal Game is a fictional browser MMO prototype. This terms draft is intended for MVP beta planning and should be legally reviewed before public or commercial launch.

## Fictional gameplay

All gameplay, economies, jobs, crimes, factions, territories, and marketplace actions are fictional strategy mechanics. The project must not be presented as real-world instruction, endorsement, or advice.

## Beta state

The MVP may be reset, rebalanced, rolled back, paused, or restricted during testing. Characters, inventories, virtual currency, factions, rankings, cosmetics, and entitlements may be corrected or reset during beta operations.

## Fair play

Players must not exploit bugs, automate gameplay, evade limits, attack the service, harass users, impersonate staff, trade accounts, scrape private data, or attempt payment/entitlement fraud.

## Moderation

Operators may remove content, apply sanctions, resolve reports, lift enforcements, adjust balances, and preserve audit records when required for safety, fairness, support, or incident response.

## Virtual items

Virtual items, cosmetics, entitlements, and game currency are limited game records. Live paid sales are disabled until the monetization layer receives legal, payment, refund, and support review.

## Pre-launch action

Before public launch, replace this draft with jurisdiction-appropriate legal text and publish it through `/terms`.
